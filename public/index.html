Percussionist, Composer, Web Developer, and Other Certain Titles

WHO AM I?

I am a percussionist and composer from Southern New Hampshire specializing in electronic and electroacoustic music. I advocate new music, and am always looking to create collaborations between creative and scientific fields.

On top of that, I am also a programmer and full-stack web developer. I enjoy building small sites and interactive web applications. My experience includes being a student web developer for the University of Connecticut, creating custom WordPress plugins and page-building modules.

Aside from that stuff, I am a huge foodie, a lover of video games and animation, and in the process of learning Japanese. (よろしくお願いします!)

WHAT CAN I DO?

Much of my experience comes from back-end development, dealing with PHP applications and WordPress development. I do have experience with creating single-page applications using Angular JS and sometimes vanilla JS. Coding things from scratch is no problem for me.
